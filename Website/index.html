About

This website helps you study using your uploaded notes. These notes can be converted into mind maps, flashcards, and quizzes, and can also build you a study planner
Built by students in Cardiff, this tool is tailored to boost your revision effectiveness.

How It Works

Upload your notes → AI analyzes them → Generates revision tools like flashcards, mind maps, and quizzes → save OR Generate a study planner to using AI
